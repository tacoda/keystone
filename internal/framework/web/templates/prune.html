heuristics in play

error
lint hard errors — missing required frontmatter, unknown kind, duplicate ids, skill without triggers, subagent without tools.
warning
lint warnings (TODO descriptions, unresolved deps/traces) and duplicate descriptions across primitives.
info
not-referenced corpus entries (no rule's traces: points at them), empty-body files (≤ 200 bytes).

Effectiveness signals ("rule was applied but had no impact") need session telemetry the runtime doesn't capture yet. Once those land, this page surfaces them too.
