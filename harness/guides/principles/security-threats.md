## IRON LAW

**Threat models go stale.** The Top 10 of 2017 is not the Top 10 of 2021, and the Top 10 of 2025 will not be the Top 10 of 2021. A security posture pinned to a single point-in-time catalog will rot. Anchor the program to the Saltzer-Schroeder principles in [[security]]; use this file as the periodically-refreshed operational view.
